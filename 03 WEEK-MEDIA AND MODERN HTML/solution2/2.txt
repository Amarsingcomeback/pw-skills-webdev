what is html entities ?

  HTML entities are special codes or characters that are used to represent reserved characters, symbols, or special characters in HTML markup. These entities allow you to display characters that have a specific meaning in HTML.

list of the entities example :-
 &lt; - less-than symbol (<)
 &amp; - ampersand (&)
 &gt; - greater-than symbol (>)
 &quot; - double quotation mark (")
 &apos; - single quotation mark (')
 &nbsp; - non-breaking space
